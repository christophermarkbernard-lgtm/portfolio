Enterprise UX Donnelly Financial Solutions

Solving for the Enterprise

A comprehensive digital transformation initiative focused on overhauling outdated financial reporting systems used by Fortune 500 companies for SEC filings and Sarbanes-Oxley compliance.

Role

Design Director

Approach

Design Thinking, Google Ventures Sprint Methodology

Duration

6 Months

Team

Cross-functional with Executive Leadership

The Challenge

Donnelly Financial's legacy systems were built decades ago, resulting in complicated, outdated tools that frustrated users and slowed down critical SEC filing processes. The company needed to consolidate business units and dozens of applications into a streamlined, modern workflow while preserving market dominance against emerging competitors.

The Process

We employed design thinking and Google Ventures Sprint methodology, bringing together executive leadership and subject matter experts in an innovation-focused sprint process. This collaborative approach helped align stakeholders and rapidly prototype solutions.

Design sprint workshop with cross-functional stakeholders

The Solution

We designed enterprise-class responsive web applications featuring inline editing, real-time reporting, document redlining with compare modes, inline commenting with context tracking, and personalized role-based workspaces. The exception-based workflow replaced function-based processes, dramatically reducing filing times.

Personalized employee workspace dashboard

Inline spreadsheet editing for SEC filings

Document editing with redline and review modes

Contextual commenting and collaboration

Real-time filing performance and analytics dashboard

Results & Impact

Weeks → Hours Filing time reduction
Reduced Business unit R&D spend
Modernized Dozens of workflows
Improved Time on task & satisfaction

Product Design CDK Global

Converging the Application Suite

Developed Unify, a modernized unified portal to consolidate fragmented legacy applications and workflows for automobile dealerships, without requiring costly data migrations.

Role

Design Director

Challenge

Legacy DMS Modernization

Users

15,000+ Dealerships

Features

SSO, AI, Mobile

The Challenge

CDK's legacy DMS product (Drive) was powerful but had become cumbersome, outdated, and increasingly difficult to sell and maintain. Users faced 58 different login screens and inconsistent interfaces across dozens of applications.

Legacy green-screen interface

Outdated dashboard design

Complex legacy navigation

Discovery & Research

We conducted extensive field research, discovery sessions, and workshops with stakeholders to understand user pain points and workflow patterns. This informed our design validation and experimentation cycles.

The Solution

Rather than forcing data migration, Unify was created as a unified interface layer enabling all CDK applications to function within a single modern experience while preserving existing infrastructure. Features include customizable workspaces, single sign-on, light/dark mode, and AI-powered customer support.
