Creating a Destination

WebGL spatial storytelling for Gallagher Way

Role Design Lead
Company Gallagher Way / Wrigley Field
Focus WebGL, Spatial UI

The Problem

Gallagher Way, the development surrounding Wrigley Field, needed a digital experience that matched its ambition. A standard brochure site couldn't communicate the energy or scale of the space.

What We Shipped

We built a fully interactive 3D experience using WebGL, allowing users to explore the development spatially. This allowed prospective tenants and visitors to navigate venues and understand the layout in a way that static maps couldn't deliver.
