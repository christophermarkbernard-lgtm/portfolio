Product Design

Converging the Application Suite

Modernizing 58 login screens into one unified experience

Role Design Director
Company CDK Global
Users 15,000+ Dealerships

The Problem

CDK's legacy dealer management system, Drive, was fragmented across dozens of disconnected applications. Our audit uncovered that dealers faced 58 different login screens, each with its own authentication and design language.

What We Shipped

We designed Unify as an interface layer that sat on top of the existing infrastructure. Dealers got one login, one workspace, and one consistent interface, without requiring a massive backend rewrite.

The experience included customizable workspaces, single sign-on, and a fully responsive mobile experience, proving that complex legacy applications could be modernized through a smart interface layer.

Results

96% Beta adoption rate
58 to 1 Login consolidation
