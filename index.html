About Me

Designing the future of enterprise software

I am a design leader at Adobe shaping the future of the Experience Cloud, specifically focused on Customer Journey Management. As enterprise software shifts from task-based workflows to operational outcome engines, my work centers on Strategic Intent Orchestration, designing the agentic cockpits and governance models powered by Real-Time CDP and unified customer profiles. My career has always integrated storytelling, business, design, and technology, evolving from my early roots as a motion and graphic designer in film and video.

Throughout my career, I've scaled design from execution to executive strategy. At IBM, I established my creative leadership in customer experience strategy as one of the founding creative directors in the Chicago studio. At Microsoft, I built the design ecosystem for the Expression Studio Suite, helping customers ship products using emerging technologies. At Truth Labs, I led a specialized design studio that doubled its revenues under my tenure. At CDK Global, as Head of Design, I elevated the discipline's impact at the executive level.

I hold a Master of Science in Communication from Illinois Institute of Technology and a Master of Design Methods from the Institute of Design, where I later taught as an adjunct professor.

My Story

A career at the intersection of design & technology

My career has been shaped by a single driving question: how do we create digital experiences that truly serve people? This journey has taken me from the precision of frame-by-frame storytelling to the high-stakes energy of live production, and eventually into Fortune 500 boardrooms, always at the intersection of storytelling, business, design, and technology.

The Beginning

Film & Motion Design

I began my career as a motion and graphic designer in film and video production. This foundation in visual storytelling taught me that design isn't just about aesthetics, it's about communicating ideas and evoking emotions. Those early lessons in narrative structure and visual communication continue to inform my approach to digital product design today.

IBM

Founding Creative Director

At IBM, I established my creative leadership in web design and customer experience strategy. As one of the founding creative directors in IBM's Chicago studio, I helped build a design practice from the ground up, defining processes, mentoring talent, and delivering enterprise solutions at scale. This experience taught me how to navigate complex organizational structures while maintaining creative excellence.

Education

Institute of Design & IIT

I hold a Master of Science in Communication from Illinois Institute of Technology and a Master of Design Methods from the Institute of Design (ID). ID's rigorous approach to human-centered design and systems thinking fundamentally shaped my practice. I later returned as an adjunct professor, teaching the next generation of designers how to tackle complex problems through structured innovation methods.

Microsoft

Expression Studio Suite

Ready to apply my skills as a systems thinker and design advocate, I joined Microsoft to work on Expression Studio Suite, a competitor to Adobe Creative Suite tailored for UX designers. I built the design ecosystem for the UX community and helped Microsoft customers ship products using Microsoft's emerging technology and design platforms. This role provided valuable experience in business development, collaborating with Silicon Valley startups and companies across consumer and enterprise domains. It further honed my storytelling skills, creating presentations and events for organizations including SXSW, IxDA, SCAD, SAIC, Kellogg, HBR, TedX, and Service Design Week.

Truth Labs

Design Studio Leadership

As the leader of Truth Labs' Chicago design studio, I took on the challenge of running a design consultancy, balancing creative vision with business realities. Under my leadership, we doubled revenues while maintaining the quality and craft that defined the studio's reputation. Working across diverse clients and industries expanded my ability to adapt design thinking to any context.

CDK Global

Head of Design

As Head of Design at CDK Global, I elevated design's impact at the executive level. Working within a complex enterprise environment serving the automotive industry, I built design systems, established research practices, and championed user-centered approaches in a traditionally technology-driven organization. This role reinforced my belief that design leadership is as much about organizational change as it is about craft.

Adobe

AI Systems Architecture

At Adobe, I lead design for Customer Journey Management within Experience Cloud, one of the most advanced AI-orchestrated product surfaces in digital marketing and enterprise software. My focus has expanded from product and systems design to pioneering a new model of design leadership: using AI to build AI-driven experiences, while keeping design thinking as the connective tissue that makes those systems human, coherent, and trustworthy at scale.
